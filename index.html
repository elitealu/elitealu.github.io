Essential FAQs

What makes EliteAlu systems superior to standard commercial fabricators?

EliteAlu utilizes certified 6063-T6 architectural grade aluminium, featuring thicker structural profiles, premium European multipoint hardware integration, and a minimum 25-micron marine-grade finish. Every project is engineering-stamped to exceed local building wind load and acoustic codes.

How long does a typical window and door fabrication process take?

Standard fabrication timelines span 4 to 6 weeks from site measurement and blueprint confirmation. Highly complex projects using fully customized dyes, bent glass, or rare custom colors can take up to 8 weeks.

What is a "Thermal Break" and why is it highly recommended?

A thermal break is a continuous composite polyamide bar installed inside the aluminium frame profile. It stops thermal energy from conducting through the metal frame, keeping heat outside during summers and locking warmth inside during winter, drastically lowering HVAC energetic bills.

Do you offer double and triple glazing for acoustic isolation?

Yes. We configure dual and triple structural laminated glass setups with specialized PVB interlayers designed specifically for noise dampening, which achieves up to STC 48 ratings - perfect for properties located close to airport flight zones or high-highway transit areas.
