Our Foundations

The Legacy of EliteAlu

EliteAlu grew from a small high-precision metal workshop established in 1998.

As structural architecture shifted toward sweeping frameless systems and massive heights, heavy aluminum emerged as the backbone. We invested early in CNC multi-axis milling systems.

Today, EliteAlu serves premium residential estates and corporate high structures across the country, holding certified compliance stamps and dynamic testing limits.
